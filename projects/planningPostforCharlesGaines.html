Planning for Charles Gains

TouchDesigner

Concept & Intent

I decided that I wanted to work with Charles Gaines' artwork titled untitled (1969-1972). It features a series of circles in different colors. When I first saw the artwork, it reminded me of hypnotic black-and-whie circular patterns, similar to optical illusions.

Art By Charles Gaines

ArtWork

The art work I choose to Inspire me by Charles Gaines

My Scene

I will be using circles,similar to Gaines work, and experimenting with their sizes.Each circle will also overlap, inspired by how colors in his piece are layered on top of one another. However, I plan to incorporate my own video content and create my own color combinations.

My Network Flow

I plan to use Movie FIle In TOP, Composite TOP, Ramp TOP, and Replicator TOP.
