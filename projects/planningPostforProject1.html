Planning for Project 1

Temperature of Taste

TouchDesigner February 27, 2026

Concept & Intent

For this project, I will be using an ice cream dataset, focusing specifically on Breyers products to keep the visualization cohesive and manageable. Visually, I am building the project around shades of blue to reflect the cold, frozen nature of ice cream. The blue tones will shift in intensity as the user interacts with the temperature control, maintaining a calm and cool atmosphere throughout the experience.
